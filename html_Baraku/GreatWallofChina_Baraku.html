The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is one of the most famous structures in the entire world. It is a series of fortifications that were built as early as 7th Century BCE. The goal, was to protect the Chinese people from other neighbouring tribes.
It is the longest man-made structure and in 2007 it was officially declared as a New World Wonder.
