Information about the Great Wall of China

General information about The Great Wall of China

The purpose of The Great Wall of China was to defend their territory from different neighbor tribes. It is believed that the construction began as early as the 7rd century BC and was constantly enlarged and strengthened till the 17th century. The Great Wall of China did not only serve as a way to defend against invasion it also played a major role in China's economic development. Created safe trade routes for the Chinese population.
It is considered the longest man-made structure in the world. The total length of The Great Wall is 21,196 km. It is believed that over 2 million people contributed in building the Great Wall of China.
In 1987 the Great Wall of China became a UNESCO World Heritage Site and in 2007 it was officially declared as one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Intriguing things to try when visiting The Great Wall of China

Great Wall Marathon

Are you a person that enjoys physical activities and challenges, if so then you must try the Great Wall Marathon. This is a once in a lifetime unique experience that you can participate on 16th of September 2023. If you think the marathon is too difficult no worries, runners of all levels can take on the challenge. Depending on your experience and training you can choose one of these 3 options: The Full Marathon (42.2 km), The Half Marathon (21.2 km), or The Fun Run (8.5 km).

Camping on The Great Wall

If you are an outdoorsy person than this is a must try. Spending a night camping in The Great Wall is a surreal experience. Looking down from a Great Wall ridge, listening to sounds in the wild. You can experience how China's lookout felt keeping watch many centuries ago.
Best season for camping is early autumn and best section is Jiankou if you are lucky enough you can at night also see the milky way.

Learn more

Go for a ride

If you find hiking up The Great Wall to difficult or you just want to try something different well you can. You can use cable cars to take in all the scenic views around The Great Wall without feeling tired or if you prefer something more interesting you can also use the toboggan roll down The Great Wall in a fun and interesting way while experiencing the great views.
